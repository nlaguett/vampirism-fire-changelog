# Vampirism Fire 8.0

---

## Bug Fixes

---

## General

- Disabled **wood** sharing for Humans.
- Fixed the countdown timer for mode selection and race selection.
- Players can no longer buy items from other players’ shops.
- Pressing **Escape** now clears popup messages such as `-c` or `-clear`.
- Reworked the anti tower damage sharing function.

---

## Architect

- **Ruby Wall** and **Beryl Wall** can no longer be upgraded into **Diamond Wall**.
- **Mini Gold Mine** count is now correctly displayed on the flag.
- **The Casino**
  - Hotkey changed from **T → C**
- **Wall Tower**
  - Now requires **Boulder Tower**
- **Pink Healing Tower**
  - Model changed to **Sacrificial Pit**
- Updated multiple building models.

---

## Humans

- Human builder no longer has an infinite **bounce** issue on spawn.

- The Jaina priority fixed and is now same as the other main builders.

---

## Orc

- **Sword of Resonance**
  - Can now be used on Orc **Comet** and **Meteor**
- **Chrysoprase Wall**
  - Fixed repair time
- **Arcane Tower**
  - Priority changed from **4 → 20**
- Fixed a bug where **Toolkit** was replaced by **Magic Potion** when used.
- Fixed a bug where Orc Slayers spawned **800 HP** engineers instead of **1600 HP**.

---

## Vampires

- **Curse of Doom**
  - Can now be cast on:
    - Tauren Harvesters
    - Orc Super Tower Builder
    - Architect Super Tower Builder
- **Path of Destruction**
  - Fixed and reimplemented

---

## Balance Changes

---

## Main Changes

- **Tome of Experience** removed.
- Picking up a gold coin now grants **1 level**.

---

# Human

- **Citadel Of Faith** now give 280 food instead of 250.

---

## Vampires

- **Claws of Attack**
  - **Big Claws** gold cost increased from **20 → 28**
- **Reaper**
  - Newly spawned Reapers are now automatically selected.
  - Reapers now have higher priority than Vampires.
- **Path of Destruction**
  - No longer affects **Jade** and **Emerald** walls.
- Vampires can no longer kill **Main Builders** before **minute 6**.

- **Sphere of Doom** cost increased from 700g -> 800g

---

## Architect

- **Diamond Wall** removed.
  - **Ruby Wall** and **Beryl Wall** can now only upgrade into **Quartz Wall**.
- **Zero Tower**
  - Damage per hit reduced from **25 → 20**
  - Attack speed reduction adjusted from **1.3 → 1**
- **Lumber Mill**
  - Lumber rewards adjusted:
    - Level 1: **50 → 100**
    - Level 2: **150 → 250**
    - Level 3: **300 → 500**
    - Level 4: **1000 → 1500**
- **Aqua Outpost**
  - Lumber cost increased from **7,500 → 40,000**
- **Boulder Tower**
  - Build time reduced from **10 → 0**

- **Pink Healing Tower**
  - Hotkey changed T -> E

- Upgraded from **Pink Diamond Tower**
- Requirement: **Pink Healing Essence** (upgrade located in ARC)
