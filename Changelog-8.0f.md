## Architect

## Orc

- **Engineers Vitality** (Research)
  - Base lumber cost reduced to **0**
  - Lumber increment increased to **10,000**
  - Base gold cost reduced to **0**
  - Gold increment reduced to **0**
  - Research now has **5 levels**
- **Toolkit** (Engineers)
  - HP reduced from **1600 → 800**
  - Gold cost increased from **3g → 5g**
  - Lumber cost increased from **10,000 → 20,000**
- **Frost Tower**
  - Attack type changed from **Chaos → Normal**
